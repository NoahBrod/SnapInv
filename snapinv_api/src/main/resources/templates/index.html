Features
↧

Multiscan

Change quantity of item by scanning the same code multiple times on the fly. Scanned code not found? It will create a new item with that code.

Search and Filters

Customizable search for the name, id, and barcode of an item. Change filters for to list the items in your invenotry easier.

Graph Tracking

Track the stock of items on a customizable chart. Sets stock thresholds and get an alert when they are hit.

Offline Capability

Use the app while offline with most functionality available. Inventory will be updated once app is back online.

About
↧

Built for Small Businesses, Designed to Scale

Your inventory, your business, your way—made easier than ever.

Making Inventory Simple, Affordable, and Accessible

Our mission is to redefine inventory management—making it easier, faster, and more accessible for small businesses, contractors, and growing teams. Unlike expensive, complex systems built for large corporations, we focus on affordable, user-friendly solutions that empower businesses of all sizes.

Inspired by Real-World Challenges

This app was born out of firsthand experience. While working with my dad, a contractor who isn’t tech-savvy, I saw how difficult it was to track materials. Many small businesses face the same issue—relying on memory, outdated spreadsheets, or costly systems that don’t fit their needs. Seeing this gap, I set out to create a simple, powerful, and affordable inventory solution.

Built for Small Businesses—Designed to Grow

We understand that many inventory tools are priced out of reach for small businesses. That’s why we’re designing this app to work for businesses at any stage, whether you’re a solo contractor or managing a warehouse. Our goal is to scale with you—offering robust features that help you grow without breaking the bank.

What Sets Us Apart?

  • Affordable for Small Businesses – We prioritize the needs of small businesses rather than just big industrial clients.
  • Multiscan Functionality – Easily scan multiple items to adjust inventory quickly—no need to dig through settings.
  • Community-Driven Development – Your feedback shapes our features, making this app built for you, with you.

Where We Are & Where We’re Going

We’re currently in alpha testing, fine-tuning the core functionality. The alpha release will be basic, but early adopters will get a lifetime license at an unbeatable price. We’re rapidly developing new features, and big updates will roll out as we grow.

Join Us Early & Shape the Future

This isn’t just an app—it’s a long-term investment in making inventory management seamless. As an early adopter, you’ll help guide development, get exclusive access to new tools, and never worry about future costs.
